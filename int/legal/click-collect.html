Loewe "Click and Collect" reservation conditions

In cooperation with our Loewe partner dealers, we offer you the simple and convenient option of reserving items directly from your chosen partner dealer using the "Click & Collect" function, which is non-binding for you. In this case, we provide the reservation service as a service provider for you and only assume the role of an intermediary. You will only conclude the purchase contract with your chosen partner retailer in the shop.

2. Scope of application

2.1 The following provisions apply to all reservations made using the "Click & Collect" reservation function in Loewe's digital shop window. The reservation function is aimed exclusively at persons of unlimited legal capacity. Persons without or with limited legal capacity require the consent of their legal representative to make reservations.

2.2 We accept reservations for our partner dealers exclusively via the electronic reservation function. Unfortunately, reservations by telephone are not possible.

3. procedure of the non-binding reservation

3.1 You can make a non-binding reservation for the desired product using the reservation form at your chosen branch of our Loewe partner dealer. You will find the contact details of your chosen partner dealer when you enter your postcode within your order form on the "Click & Collect" landing page. In order to make the reservation, we need your name, your e-mail address and your mobile phone number for any queries.

3.2 Immediately after you have made your reservation, we will send you an email confirming receipt of your reservation with your personal reservation number. We will immediately forward your reservation request to your selected Loewe partner dealer for processing. Your selected Loewe partner dealer will then contact you using the contact details provided for the further procedure.

3.3 The product will be reserved for you to collect from your selected partner dealer for a period of two business days. The period begins when you send your online reservation. You can collect the product from our partner retailer within the reservation period using your reservation number and pay on site. The binding purchase contract will only be concluded between you and the Loewe partner dealer in the shop on site.

3.4 If you do not collect the product within the reservation period, the reservation will be automatically cancelled. In this case, you must expect that our partner dealer will sell the product elsewhere in the normal course of business.

4. Cancellation of the reservation (possible at any time)

4.1 By using the reservation function, you do not make a binding contractual declaration to purchase the reserved product. You can cancel your reservation at any time. To do so, please contact ccc@loewe.de or use the cancellation option in our confirmation e-mail.

4.2 If, in exceptional circumstances, the product you have reserved is no longer available from the partner retailer (e.g. as a result of a prior sale immediately after you have sent your reservation), the Loewe partner retailer will inform you of this immediately and arrange a new date for you to collect the product. In this case, your reservation will also be deemed to have been cancelled.

To carry out the reservation, we need your full name, your e-mail address and your mobile phone number for any queries.
