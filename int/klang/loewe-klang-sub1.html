Loewe klang sub1
Due to the simplest plug-and-play installation, the Loewe klang sub1 is immediately ready for use with any Loewe television from the SL3 series onwards – for the ultimate in audio enjoyment.
Discover a 2.1 audio system with full bass for a more dynamic sound. The unique feature of latency-free Bluetooth connection allows you to pair the compact subwoofer with your TV. The result is a perfectly balanced sound experience for maximum listening pleasure without sound delay, annoying buzzing and disturbing wires between the subwoofer and TV.

It has a small size and big performance thanks to the innovative audio technology inside, the Loewe klang sub1 is only half the size of devices with a conventional subwoofer design. This means that this space-saving powerhouse fits perfectly into smaller living rooms without any kind of vibration. By the way, if you own a third TV with a regulated audio output, it's possible to connect the Loewe klang sub1 to it.

Wireless sound enjoyment.

Thanks to excellent German engineering and the latency-free Bluetooth connection, the Loewe klang sub1 couples wirelessly with the TV. It'sfree of any sound delay and without any annoying buzzing meaning you can enjoy a perfectly coordinated sound experience.

Strong without vibrations.

An absolutely oscillation-free and, therefore, vibration-free sound due to the unique arrangement of the speaker chassis and passive radiators, the Loewe klang sub1 ensures that persons nearby are not disturbed by vibration noise.

Sophisticated, compact design.

Its innovative, sophisticated and compact design and selected, high-quality materials make the Loewe klang sub1 a space-saving, stylish powerhouse for smaller living spaces compared to conventional subwoofers.

Dynamic and powerful.

Upgrade to a 2.1 system

The Loewe klang sub1 simply brings more dynamics to the sound and upgrades the TV to a 2.1 sound system with full bass. Experience full, voluminous bass and expand your audio enjoyment.
 

Instant audio enjoyment.

Installing the Loewe klang sub1 couldn't be easier.Via plug-and-play and just one menu setting, the power subwoofer can be connected immediately to all Loewe televisions from the SL3 series onwards and third-party TVs with regulated TV output.
