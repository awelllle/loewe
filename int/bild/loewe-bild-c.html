Loewe bild c

The Loewe bild c provides a unique viewing experience with picture worlds of the highest quality through the latest generation Loewe exclusive Edge LED panels.
 
 The UHD resolution optimized by the Loewe experts in combination with sophisticated HDR image processing in the 43 inch model or the Full HD model in 32 inch create images in perfect balance for the highest watching pleasure.

 Experience nuanced colours, realistic sharpness and smooth transitions that will make your eyes relax.

Smart perfection.

A television for all those who value design and sustainability, but do not want to do without anything. With the Loewe bild c, you get a compact format that packs a lot into it.
 

Powerful Sound.

The front-firing sound system with bass reflex box and 60 watts total music power provides warm low tones, is room-filling and opens a precise stereo stage.

Compact table stand solution.

The Loewe table stand c in the elegant surface chrome is included with the new Loewe bild c.
 The table stand is turn-able and the cable routing is guided along the stand neck. 

New chassis, new possibilities.

Thanks to the new Loewe SL7 chassis, the new Loewe os7 software as well as the new Loewe home screen in combination with the Smart System and the App Store from VIDAA, the Loewe bild c offers a broad range of application possibilities, both at present and in the future.

Whether it's Netflix, Amazon Prime or YouTube - a large number of streaming services are already natively integrated in the Loewe home screen and are accessible at the touch of a button using the dedicated direct buttons on the new Loewe remote control. Quick and direct access to all popular VOD services is guaranteed.
