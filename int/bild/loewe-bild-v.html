Loewe bild v

Experience a world where elegance, tradition and performance come together in perfect harmony and discover the extraordinary TV models in the bild v line from Loewe.

The latest technology and a 4K OLED panel with HDR10, HLG and Dolby Vision™ ensure maximum comfort. As you would expect from Loewe, each set is individually calibrated and optimised for perfect viewing pleasure before delivery.

The soundbar with six drivers provides 80 watts of total music output and, with its basalt coloured fabric cover, blends perfectly into the living environment.
German engineering with a great love of detail - for fascinating TV moments. 

OLED Technology.

The OLED technology stands for bright and natural colours, for perfect contrast - from deepest black to pure white - and for detailed sharpness even in very fast movements. In addition to impressive black and the finest nuances in dark parts of the picture, the OLED technology of the Loewe bild v enables brilliant colour reproduction. This is because against a pitch-black background, colours appear particularly bright and vivid. This makes the screens ideal for HDR films and content with Dolby Vision, which contain more and finer graded colours and a wider range of brightness. 
 

Sound experience.

The powerful stereo sound system with closed bass reflex box and six integrated drivers generates 80 watts of voluminous total music power. In addition, the soundbar provides warm bass tones and fills the room with rich sound. The intelligent technology Mimi Defined™ recognises the optimum for the ears. Sound and tone are analysed in real time and the sound of the TV is adjusted to your personal hearing profile.  

Record - Take away.

With the DR+ built-in hard drive, you can record for hours. With a storage capacity of 1TB, your Loewe bild v will be right on schedule: You can record two programmes at the same time and watch TV on another channel. The TV programme can be paused and continued at a later time with a time delay. With the Loewe app, it is also possible to watch the programme on your mobile device while you are on the move.
